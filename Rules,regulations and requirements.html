Rules & Requirements

The following are the requirements for application
  1. Must be aged between 18-26 years

  3. If you are late(past 7:40)a 3000/= will be asked of you

  5. An ADN card containing all your personal information(ID,Baptisimal card,Passport photo,etc.)is also required


  6. Coordinators are also ALLOWED to inquire your Id to show you are the true ownwer of the ADN card

    7. Inputing both will allow us confirm on your behalf to ease the program
